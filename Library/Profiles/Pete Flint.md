# Pete Flint

## Overview

General Partner at NFX. NFX invested in Veras' seed round. Active board member and strategic advisor across numerous board meetings (2023-2025).

## Key Advice

- Enterprise deals driven by making people look good
- Valuation: doesn't worry about valuation, $40M is fine, don't push too high to avoid down-rounds
- Check sizing: cap at $250k per check, maybe 4 partners/investors max
- No discount on product subscription
- Customer investors approach: "Our customers love our product so much, they want to invest"
- EOR recommendations and resources
