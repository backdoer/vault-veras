# Pete Meeting on Strategic Investors

Advice:
- Get other customers to invest as well
- Maybe cap out at $250k per check (maybe up to 4 partners/investors)
- Don't worry too much about the valuation - $40M is just fine. Also, don't push the valuation too high because it can cause a down-round later and you want them bought in and excited anyways
- "Our customers love our product so much, they want to invest"
- No discount on product subscription

Concerns:
- Them thinking they can dictate the roadmap (keep the check size small)
- Competitors seeing it as a conflict (have other customers participate)
