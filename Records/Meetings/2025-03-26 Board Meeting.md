# Board Meeting

1.2 million residents (going to double in the next 5-10 years)
5 hrs per care assessment
Average length stay is 30 days (turns over 12 times a year)
14 million MDS assessments a year
70,000,000 hours/year
$40/hr average wage
$2.8B spent on MDS assessments a year
15,000 facilities
$186,000/year spent in labor cost a year per facility
Each facility is doing ~950 MDS assessments a year
Average cost of an MDS assessment is ~$200
If we took 10% of that ($20/assessment), that's ~$20,000/year per facility
That's a $300,000 TAM

To have a billion dollar TAM in the SNF industry, we need to be able to extract about $65,000/year per facility. Including AL about doubles that.
