# Timbri Hurst Interview - Southern Fabrication Works, LLC

90 employees
Creates machines for the food industry
Looking to hire 5-6 welders in the near future

Employee referrals range from $50-200

Using Indeed - paying ~$150-300/month. It's been months since they've gotten any applicants from Indeed. She bumped it to $300/month and she got 2 applicants.

They send out offer letters to half of the applicants and then half of those actually get hired.

Struggling to find welders.

Developing their own apprenticeship program to try to attract more talent at a younger age - have to be 18.

Loves the idea of pay per hire - doesn't currently know how much she'd pay for a certified, qualified welder. Will talk to supervisor.

School relationships: CSI (College of Southern Idaho), BYU-I. She sits on the advisory board of the welding program of CSI.
