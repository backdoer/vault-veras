# Meeting with Stanbridge University ()

They like to take a proactive approach from day 1 and make sure they're keeping touchpoints/check-ins along the way. 3-4 check-ins during cohort (resume review, mock interviews, job lead sharing, etc.)

Centralized communication across admins/instructors/etc. would be awesome. 

Timelines differ per program.

Programs are constantly starting all year in any given month

They said everything exports and above would be necessary for V1 with the most weight going into proactive prompts.

They'd like to be able to filter/group by:
- Cohort
- Program
- Campus
- Job leads sent out

They would eventually really like an employer CRM to be able to see all the employer relationships, the students related to those employers (employed, lead shared, externship, etc.) and be able to manage it all in one place. This falls in step with our vision of owning the placement process eventually.

They really like the idea of having a job board tied into the process and see/love the longterm vision of tying placement into placement tracking. 

They're open to giving us an integration to their in-house SIS - they could do REST APIs and webhooks. The plan is to go with import/export for V1 then move to integration if they're loving it. Integration is not a must-have.

Their Spreadsheet of Tracking:
Career office they're assigned to
goal of meeting
Date of meeting
what happened during meeting
email
LinkedIn URL

Open to getting another meeting on the calendar in 2 weeks.
