# Product Process

Goal:
Implement process to lower value, viability, feasibility, and usability risk by doing proper discovery across customers, internal stakeholders, the market, and the data. 

To do that, we need to strong, collaborative team with high trust who can solve these problems together. 

Which brings up 2 topics:
1. Loss of trust 
2. Process changes 


Loss of trust with the team:
- political 
   - kissing up, kicking down (Power dynamics)
   - taking credit for ideas
- strong opinions not backed by good discovery
- more talking than listening

Product Development Lifecycle (high risk):
- Lower risk across the 4 product dimensions
- Discovery process (PRD) led by Brad
- You own the process of PRD to finalized designs
   - validation with:
      - product
      - sales
      - cs 
      - customers
      - engineering
- Build owned by engineering
- Validation and success metrics owned by Brad
- EPD collaborates through all 3 stages

Process circumvention (low risk):
- bugs 
- low risk, simple tasks
