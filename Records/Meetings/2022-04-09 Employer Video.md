# Employer Video

1. Schools need to stay accredited 

To do that, they need to place their students and report on that placement. That’s where we come in. We help schools place their students and get visibility into the process.

We’ve identified 4 primary methods for collecting that placement data:
1. Employment funnel
2. Direct outreach to students
3. Direct outreach to employers 
4. Integrations with government agencies 

We’ve prioritized the first of these 4 as a starting point and plan to move into the other 3 soon. 

Employment funnel:
1. Employers to post jobs
2. Students to apply to jobs
3. Visibility into metrics

We currently drive to the application phase of the funnel then make a handoff though we plan to move further down the funnel soon. 

#1 - employers to post
1. Embedded job board 
2. Drip campaigns to employer lists from school 

#2 - students to apply
1. Schools import students 
2. Students get texted job matches w/ the ability to set preferences on pay and location

#3 - visibility into metrics 
1. Applicant tracking system and incentive to report 
2. Reporting dashboard for schools 

Next steps:
- make the need to report back on status front and center in product - tie it to employer reputation 
- move further down the recruitment funnel and provide scheduling and interviewing tooling
- collect from the other 3 placement data sources to really become sticky with schools and give a wholistic placement picture
