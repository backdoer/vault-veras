# Pricing Board Meeting

SAAS vs A la carte (Cadence)

Top of the funnel vs bottom of the funnel (Funnel Positioning)
- Pay per hire (recruiter model) vs pay per text/view/application

Employer Initiated vs. Job Seeker Initiated (Proactivity)
- Sponsored vs Campaigned

Constraints:
- There are more jobs/employers than there are job seekers
   - The further up the funnel we are, the more love there is to spread around
- Attribution gets harder down the funnel
- Risk gets passed to us down the funnel as it's dependent on their process
- SAAS revenue is more predictable

Other components:
- Freemium model
- Embedded tools (ATS, interaction management, reporting, resume resurfacing)
    - Do we try to chase one of these markets?
- No one in the industry quite has it figured out
- Price decoys
- Annual discounts 
- How do we account for seasonality? Credits that build up over time

Do pay-per-hire after the network is more established and to better align incentives with good-actor customers.

Labor marketplaces work best when embedded. That's why NFX was most excited about JobWise. How can embed ourselves with employers?
