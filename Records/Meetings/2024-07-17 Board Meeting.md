# Board Meeting

"Play to win - don't play not to lose."

When you're hiring sales and CS ground level employees, it can be best to hire 2 at a time. Get 100 resumes, interview the best 10, hire 2.
- It can help you get a diversity of perspective.
- It can lower onboarding expense.
- Can create camaraderie.
- When you get bigger, you hire "classes" of people (e.g. 6 people starting this date).

"Speed is your #1 asset, so don't compromise speed. Would rather error on the side of too fast and a bit of instability."
