# Call with Michael

Apple Valley Rehab. 12 building organization. 

95 bed facility. 150 employees. 

Their scheduler also handles supplies.

He wants to cut overtime and double time. He was able to get rid of agency through overtime/double time.

They use a florence nightingale schedule. Different from 4-2.

He prefers to not hire people who want a ton of flexibility - but sometimes, out of necessity, he will because it's better than agency and overtime.

He would like to be able to schedule physical, occupational, speech, and respirational therapists. Physical therapists are the most important. He said "Rehab Therapist" is not really a thing.

He uses UKG.

He was pretty anchored on whether or not lunch was included in the shift. 

He has 5 areas (he calls them stations)

The colors make it feel a little busy.

He was thinking the color could mean the type of employee (PRN, FT, agency, etc). 

Reports:
- Loves the overtime and PPDs
- With call offs, he wants to be able to drill down and see who the culprits are
- He'd like to be able to see the CNA/RN/LPN PPD. He also said the overall nursing (LVN/RN) PPD is useful.

He tells his staff to check in 5-7 minutes before the shift. He doesn't want them checking in too early because it increases overtime and pay.

He likes the idea of automating but he thinks someone will need to be there regardless helping steer.
