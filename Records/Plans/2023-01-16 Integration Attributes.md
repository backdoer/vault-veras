# Integration Attributes

Priority of integrations:
- Build them as soon as the opportunity presents itself

- Recurring
- Tied back to SIS

The SIS' are sleepy and bureaucratic and so are the schools. So we just want to make it as simple and seamless as possible. 

The SIS integration is really the holy grail and allows us to:
1) Get a school integrated in minutes with a recurring, automated integration
2) Market to the schools via the SIS partnership
3) Leaves open the option to do writebacks to their SIS
4) Have automatic access to alumni

The next best thing is having them automate pulling a standard report from their SIS and putting it on an FTP server for us to pull. This allows us to:
1) Set up a recurring, automated integration with as little effort as possible done by the school

The last resort is to simply have them upload a flat file. This gets students into the system, but it's not recurring and it relies on them needing to consistently come back.
