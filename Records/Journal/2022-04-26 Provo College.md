# Provo College

Medical based.
Constant communication with graduates. 
Exit interview prior to graduation to ensure they have the best contact information. They reach out to them every other week to check in.
Help with resume, mock interviews, etc.
Phone call, text message (TrueDialog), Email
185 students they're currently tracking. 30 more will be graduating at the end of the month. 
In a cohort year, they're right around 205 (pretty small, relatively)
ABHES is their accrediting body (as opposed to COE).
Web Client Anthology (they're moving to web/cloud based on Anthology) is where all of their communications are stored - their SIS.
They have an amazing VP who's all about training for staff.
Provo College is part of a larger, systemized conglomerate. 
Verification of employment w/ employer - career advancement attestation (people who were previously in a job but are getting promoted)
3 ways to confirm: 1) through graduate (docusign) 2) through employer (docusign) 3) Third party verification (Equifax, Thomas & Company, TrueWork)
Half through the verifier, and the other half is through the graduate or employer
They work heavily with their students in the last semester (especially with VSN students) for employment. There's a career prep class. Help with resumes, mock interviews, etc. 5 touch points in that last semester. They fill out a form letting them know whether they're currently looking or already have a job lined up.
Part of Unitech Learning (all about compliance)
Decision could be made by corporate team across all Unitech
Hands-on, personal touch is a big deal 
Career services 100% owns the process for placement, communicating w/ employers, etc.
Small amount of students fall through the cracks. Those who get lost are those who aren't passing the test. Then they fall off the map.
Programs (Provo College):
- BSN
- Physical Therapist Assistance Program (PTA)
ABHES standard is 70% & internal goal is 85% (they want to do better than standard)
They're all about results
