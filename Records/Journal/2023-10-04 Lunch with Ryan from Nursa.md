# Lunch with Ryan from Nursa

Product was garbage.

Make onboarding really smooth.

Their set rate was 2% on the phone.

Their marketing strategy strategy was garbage on both sides.

They didn't have CS - they would have e2e sales and sales would be paid commission on consumption.

They used indeed to find supply.

Hyperlocal worked better than cold calls. In Seattle, they couldn't sign anyone for a month on the phone. They sent someone out and signed up 7 in 2 days..

Their receivables were often bad. Practices would push back and/or not pay.

You want the ability to monitor and optimize supply and demand within specific geos. E.g. the ability to run promotions.

Everyone was hardcore multi-tenanting. Practices and Clinicians. 

He said that Nursa was the best product-market fit he's ever seen.
