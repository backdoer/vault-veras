# Matrixcare

They have APIs that give back the census, and we can do the count.

API:
- Census snapshot
- ADT feed (no more than 5 minute lag)
- They don't require their customers to pay anything
- They have a marketplace
- They have a newsletter that announces partners

They have 7 different CMRs. Iris is on MatrixCare Senior Living. Their most predominant one is MatrixCare 360 (which is SNF).

They charge:
- For the certification of every API
- $2,000 for each API against each EMR
- Ongoing charge of $40/month per facility for all the endpoints
- That includes being in the marketplace
- They do webinars
