- user going from application to application

They manage contracts, BAAs, etc. within the confines of Z-PAX.

They currently have 1800 users of the applications

CMI Connect will be the first application on there

Z-PAX isn't a product, in and of itself, it's a broker to other applications

Z-PAX doesn't consider themselves an advertising site - they consider themselves a developer efficiency tool

- PCC data relay
- SSO
- Payroll APIs
- Z-Intel (all of the data they've scraped from the public domain)
