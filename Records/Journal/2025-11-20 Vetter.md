Employees are currently submitting "green sheets" for timesheet corrections and then Ed has all of the payroll admins at each facility go in and make the changes

Most facilities usually have a designated computer for clocking in

They'd prefer to maintain a uniform way for people to clock in (facial or pin)

Their employee codes are 6 digits long and they're unique

5 or less people, on average, are needing to use the fallback method on a kiosk or computer

Vetter currently assigns one job code to a staff, but they can work other codes. If someone is working a different job code, vetter wants them to be able to pick it when they clock in. Today, they see the whole list of codes - no association. 

Workers get different rates based on the facility and job code. That is something that is NOT currently supported in ADP. Validating for the structure of our system!

They currently don't have a lot of flexibility around picking the facility when clocking in. Should we make them pick a facility?

They use PointClickCare for their general ledger. The cost number is the link between PCC and ADP. It includes the job code, the department code, and the facility code.
