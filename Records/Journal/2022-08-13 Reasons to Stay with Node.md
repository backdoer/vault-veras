# Reasons to Stay with Node

- End to end type safety 
- Ability to have hiring flexibility
- Momentum and progress 
- Less overhead (deployments, patterns, tests, repos, app interfaces, app interface tests, linters, formatters, text editor configs, etc.)
- Seth and Congo both love TS (so I think we'll get there)
- We can head towards a monorepo! One repo to rule them all. That seems to be the prevailing pattern right now. Seth, Travis and Congo all vouch for it. 
- Ethan also said the easier it is for us to spin up our whole infrastructure with one command, the easier our lives will be long term.
