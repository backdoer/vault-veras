# Enterprise vs SMB

Enterprise vs SMB?

SMB:
- long tail
- quicker sales cycle
- they nickel and dime
- they're more open to a point solution
- more prone to self server

Enterprise:
- big price points
- bigger demands (both in results and functionality)
- bigger price point
- more prone to an inside sales team
