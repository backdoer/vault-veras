# Scheduling Questions

Multiple areas per shift? 

PPD? Clinical vs non clinical? Employment type?

One or multiple schedulers? 

At what granularities do people schedule?

Should we treat the schedulers like 5 year olds? Give them actions trending towards the goal without telling them the goal. 

How do we display published, unpublished, assigned, unassigned?

One global draft state to rule them all?

Wage goes on shift scheduled contact? How to handle full time pay vs PRN pay? 

Shift differentials live on a shift as multiplier or fixed per hour or fixed total?
