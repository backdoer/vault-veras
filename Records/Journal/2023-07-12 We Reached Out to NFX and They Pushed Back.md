# We Reached Out to NFX and They Pushed Back

We reached out to NFX and they pushed back pretty hard saying it's highly unusual for early stage startup employees to get additional stock grants after the initial grant. They recommended waiting until we'd raised again, made significant progress, vesting is nearing completion AND a promotion.

That said, we went to bat and told them how much value you add and how you're hustling, pull long hours, getting things done across design, engineering, swag, strategy, customer calls, and more. 

We pushed to at least get you up to a very meaningful stake of 2% which is another 95,000 shares and we got that approved. All in all (including your base, health stipend, vesting equity valued at preferred price which is how much you'll hopefully be able to sell it for ONE day), that puts you right at about $300k/year fully loaded.

That said, this gets you up to a level where there likely won’t be more grants outside of the scope of what I mentioned previously: raised again, made significant progress, vesting is nearing completion AND a promotion. 

Grateful to have you!
