# Network Strategy

1. School Trust
2. TCPA
3. Job Seeker Burnout
4. Employer Value (how good is the match?)

Strategy options:
1. Slow roll the network w/ school & job seeker consent
    - This is our current strategy and it feels like it's not going as fast as desired
    - This may require a SAAS play to keep the lights on
    - We've cornered ourselves a bit w/ schools by telling them it's free AND we won't text their students
    - Schools aren't adopting the outreach tools as quickly as hoped
    - Having students go first is hard with the cold start problem (there may not be any employers yet)
2. Give the employers the reigns & put everyone in the headhunting pool
    - Employers are desperate and will definitely text students
    - Employers reaching out may drive the best conversion
    - Employers will take what they can get and will likely find value w/ the current pool
    - We can get creative with TCPA, job seeker burnout, and employer value. School trust is really the big constraint in my mind.
    - Let the demand lead - the employers will automatically bring the geo/industry demand to the table and make sure we're inviting the right people.
