# Care Level Filters

Options for filtering workers by care level:
1. Permissions 
2. Add care level to job position (use primary employment)
3. Put care level directly on worker
4. Create custom departments and/or job positions

Or just pull out the workforce management reports and don't allow filtering by care level there since it's more of a clinical constraint.

TLDR: We can filter shift-related reports by care level. If you want to filter worker reports by that, you'll need to split it out to its own department.
