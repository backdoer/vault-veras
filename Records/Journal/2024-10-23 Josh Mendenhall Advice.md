# Josh Mendenhall Advice

Try to use brand attributes that no one else can say 

90% of people will just see it and love it for what it is. Others will actually understand what you're trying to accomplish.

The best design makes them think they figured it out even though you led them down the exact path you wanted them to take.

Don't do the V next to the word Veras unless it fits into the word

Don't worry about color until you've nailed the shape

Make sure it's timeless
