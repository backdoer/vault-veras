# Pricing Takeaways

Doctor Staffing is doing the most profitable strategies for DAs but the least profitable strategy for RDHs. Though they're paying their RDHs $40/hour and charging 52.50 so that's how they can make it work. Potentially incentives us to underpay our staff. Unless we make the all-in hourly amounts variable/flexible. They seem to be paying their DAs on the high end and their RDHs on the low end. 

Doing just $60 on top of the employer's W2 hourly wage without some kind of FICA passthrough is NOT a viable strategy. 

Flat Fee + Percentage seems to be the best comprehensive strategy.
