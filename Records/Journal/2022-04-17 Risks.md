# Risks

Usability Risks:
- Schools getting the placement data they need in the way they need it
- Employers reporting back on placement (not integrated)
- Students interacting
   - Applying for jobs
   - Reporting back on placement

Feasibility Risks:
- Can we successfully integrate with school systems?
   - Read student data, certificate, enrollment start, enrollment end, graduation, etc.
- Can we successfully integrate with employer systems?
   - Read jobs out
   - Post applications in
   - Read application updates out
- Can we successfully integrate with the government (USHE, etc.)?
   - Read student job placement data

Viability Risks:
- What if we can't monetize through employers?
   - Conflict of interest monetizing student data (legislation). Fiduciary duties?
   - To get the same TAM monetizing through the 4100 schools, we’d need to charge ~$1M per school
