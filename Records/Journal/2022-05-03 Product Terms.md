Hierarchy: 
- School (led by SuperAdmin)
- Campus (led by Admin)
- Department (led by Department Director)
- Program (led by Program Head)
- Class (led by Instructor)

An organization like USHE could have access to multiple schools.
An admin at the school level could have access to all the school campuses.
An admin at the campus level could have access to all the departments.
Still need to do some more research to get a feel of the necessary permission structures.
