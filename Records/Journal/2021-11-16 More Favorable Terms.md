# More Favorable Terms

- with cause - not without cause
- acceleration clause in the event of termination without cause
- repurchase rights on unvested shares at cost (free in this case)
- no repurchase rights on vested shares - company gets right of first refusal on vested shares in the event of transfer/sale. Can sell in secondary sales at market value (what an investor will pay) which can be closer to 70-100% of preferred. There’s a conflict of interest with a 409a. 409a can be as low as 10-20% of preferred. 
- The only thing that can truly dictate a fair price is two willing parties entering into a transaction
